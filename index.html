How It Works
Getting Started
Tap any workout card on the home screen to start a session. No workouts yet? Go to Manage to build one, or tap Quick Workout for a smart suggestion.
Settings
Tap the gear icon in Manage. Change your theme, set default reps and sets, adjust weekly muscle targets, manage backups, and toggle features on or off.
Logging a Session
Enter weight and reps for each set. Tap REST to start the timer. Tap Save to save progress mid-session, or End to finish.
Building a Workout
Manage then New Workout. Tap Suggest Exercises to see exercises targeting your least-trained muscles.
Weekly Schedule
The schedule card on home lets you assign workouts to days. The dot shows weekly muscle coverage. Tap to see what is light or missing.
Health Tab
Log macros, body weight, and sleep. Use + and - to add or subtract from today's totals. Set targets in the card header.
