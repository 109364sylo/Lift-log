How It Works
Getting Started
Tap any workout card to start a session. No workouts? Go to Manage to build one, or tap Quick Workout for a smart suggestion.
Streaks
Complete 2+ sessions in a week to qualify. Hit qualifying weeks back to back to build your streak.
Logging a Session
Enter weight and reps for each set. Tap REST to start the timer. Tap the timer button on any exercise for a per-exercise countdown. Tap Save to save progress, or End to finish.
Duration Exercises
Cardio and timed exercises log seconds instead of weight/reps. Toggle this on any exercise in Manage.
Weekly Schedule
Assign workouts to days on the home screen. The dot shows weekly muscle coverage.
Health Tab
Log macros, body weight, and sleep. Use + and - to add or subtract from today's totals.
