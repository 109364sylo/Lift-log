How It Works
Getting Started
Tap any workout card on the home screen to start a session. No workouts yet? Go to Manage → New Workout to build one, or tap Quick Workout for a smart suggestion.
Settings ⚙
Tap the gear icon in the top right of Manage. Change your theme, set default reps and sets, adjust weekly muscle targets, manage backups, and toggle features on or off.
Logging a Session
Enter weight and reps for each set. Tap REST to start the rest timer — it buzzes and notifies you when done. Tap Save to save progress mid-session, or End to finish and close.
Rest Timer
Tap the timer display in the session header to change the duration. Duration can also be set in Settings.
Building a Workout
Manage → New Workout. The Programming Gaps strip at the top shows muscles underrepresented in your existing workouts — tap to add an exercise for that muscle instantly.
Weekly Schedule
The schedule card on the home screen lets you assign workouts to days. The colored dot shows your weekly muscle coverage at a glance — tap it to see which muscles are light or missing.
Health Tab
Log macros, body weight, and sleep. Tap Targets in the daily card to set protein and calorie goals. Use + and − to add or subtract from today's totals.
